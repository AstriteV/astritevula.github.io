Bio

Astrite Vula is a Twickenham-based freelance artist exploring the intersections of perspective, light, and colour through abstract and surreal forms. Her recent series of four-dimensional paintings invite the viewer into an immersive experience of tonal rhythm and shifting texture — works that evoke both the physical and the metaphysical.

Drawing from deconstructivist and poststructuralist ideas, Astrite’s practice combines traditional mediums such as oils, acrylics, and drawing tools with an intuitive sense of movement and space. Each work is a conversation between material, energy, and perception — minimal yet deeply layered.

Her background spans installation, performance, branding, and design, complemented by contributions to Arts Richmond’s committees and the Riverside Artists Group. Astrite holds a Postgraduate Diploma and BA in Fine Arts.
